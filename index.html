POC concept · design ops for AI-first orgs

AI-first design thinking — system architecture

Design thinking's principles are durable. The event itself is fundamentally humans coming together to discuss something that matters — to frame a problem, choose a direction, commit to action. What's becoming re-architectable is the meta — how those humans are supported, surrounded, and accelerated by AI; how their collective toolkit grows as they go; and how the practice itself learns over time.

Act 1 · Before

The architect spawns this workshop

Before the humans arrive, the architect takes intent from the facilitator, consults its pattern library, and composes this workshop's tuned instance — subsystem emphasis, agent personalities, lifecycle pacing, default permissions, all set per workshop. The workshop instance then prepares: org IP is indexed for live retrieval, pre-event research is synthesized into briefing material, and each participant's position-prompt responses become a delegate agent enrolled with their voiceprint. The skill exchange is seeded with the participants' contributed skills and the org's available library.

Meta · Workshop architect
